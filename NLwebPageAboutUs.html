About us

Hello all foodies! Welcome to Kolbaski Bull, our shop and bar with the motto excellent quality meat at good prices. Here you will find the raw materials and ingredients that we choose ourselves when we cook - everything from quality meat & sausages to homemade sauces, cheeses, oils, delicacies and lots more. Yes, we have everything you need to treat yourself, your family or your guests to a lovely dinner. Our bar also has a wide range of beers and an exciting drink list; feel free to take a break at the bar before or after your purchase. With us, you are welcome at After Work or a lovely dinner, we have a lot of places for spontaneous diners. Our hope is that Köttmästarn becomes a natural stop every day on the way home from work. You will find us at Tegnergatan 32, just around the corner from Grill. Welcome!
